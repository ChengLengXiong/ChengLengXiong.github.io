Role Models

By surviving the war, living in the jungle, emigrating to the United States, working two job and help/teach his kids school related, Ka Ying was able to overcome by focusing on the big picture which was to provide enough food for his family. Ka Ying was able to get enough money to buy a house for his family to live. He continue this way until when his oldest daugther and son graduate and helped out. After years pass all of his daugther and son were old enough to work and pay for the house.

His daugther and son look up to him as their role model because of his hard work and dedication for his family. Ka Ying alway teach and tell them evverytime they did something wrong. He always said, You guys should focus on your dream of what you want to do when you grow up. Don't play around too much. You guys are given the chance to study and gain knowledge in America, the free country and unlike us who are too old to go to school.

Now Ka Ying is glad that all of his daughters and sons are old enough to live their life and have a way to earn for themselve. Ka Ying is retire from working and is now watching his sons and daughters babys. In the future, Ka Ying plan to rest and stay home to watch his grandchildren and sometimes he also plan to tarvel out of the United States to visit his hometown and his relatives that couldn't come to the US. He plan to spent his day with his family.
