About

Ka Ying Xiong, a hmong refugee boy who was born in a village in Xiang Khouang, Laos, a country that bordered Thailand. Ka Ying was the youngest child of the three boys. At a very young age, he had to help his families work in the field garden to have enough food to eat. When he was about seven years old, the war broke out and they had to emigrate from their small village to somewhere safe. Ka Ying and his family safely arrived in Thailand. He got married. They stayed in Thailand for a long time until 2005, the year where they got the chance to come to the United States to seek a better life there. It was a struggle living in a new country because he didn't know the culture of the people there. Ka Ying and his wife had to work two job to provided for their families. He became a role model for his family after his his kids grew up.
